Description

"THE HOUSESITTER" is an ASCII art, text-based, horror game, in which you must spend several days and night in a remote farmhouse... alone.

In "THE HOUSESITTER" you are asked to watch the house by your friend Rita. She and her son have recently moved into a new home in the countryside, but things have not been going well for them since moving in. Rita was initally excited to renovate the house, but now she seems checked out and her son hasn't been sleeping. Now they are going out of town for a few days to clear their heads. In the meantime, you’ve been asked to watch the house and you happily oblige. However, the house may not be quite so accommodating...
