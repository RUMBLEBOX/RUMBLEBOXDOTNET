Description

MURDER MALL is a pixel art, point-and-click adventure, horror game, in which you play as a security guard working a graveyard shift at a dying mall... alone.

Normally, it's just a boring job where you periodically patrol the mall throughout the night to ensure nothing is amiss. Usually, you just sit around in the security office for hours watching TV and wait for the shift to end, but tonight something is different… During a routine walkthrough you start to feel as though you are being watched and you quickly realize that you are not alone…

Can you make it through the night?
